ℹ️ About Us

MultipleHealthPredict is an AI-powered platform designed to assist individuals in understanding their health conditions better. Our system analyzes both symptom inputs and lab test reports, comparing values with WHO-approved reference ranges to provide preliminary predictions.

Our mission is to empower users with early health insights, helping them take timely action and make informed decisions. By integrating medical datasets and global health standards, we ensure that our results are reliable, accurate, and easy to interpret.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This is a prototype tool meant for educational and awareness purposes. It is not a replacement for professional medical consultation. Always seek a doctor’s advice for diagnosis or treatment.
